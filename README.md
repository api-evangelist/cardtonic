# Cardtonic (cardtonic)
Cardtonic is a leading online platform that specializes in buying and selling gift cards. With a user-friendly interface and a seamless process, Cardtonic allows customers to easily convert their unwanted gift cards into cash or purchase discounted gift cards from top retailers.
